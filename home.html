What is a YLP Toastmasters Club Program for Children?

The Youth Leadership Program (YLP) Toastmasters Club is an educational platform specifically designed to help children develop communication and leadership skills. This program empowers young leaders through structured speeches, evaluations, and impromptu speaking opportunities.

Online Meeting Details:

Our meetings are held **online** every Sunday at 11:00 AM EST (10:00 AM CST, 8:30 PM IST).

Advantages of Participating in the YLP Toastmasters Club:

  • Develop confidence in public speaking.
  • Improve leadership and communication skills.
  • Learn how to give and receive constructive feedback.
  • Enhance critical thinking and active listening skills.
  • Prepare for real-world presentations and interviews.

Mission Statement:

The mission of a Toastmasters Club is to provide a mutually supportive and positive learning environment in which every member has the opportunity to develop communication and leadership skills, which in turn foster self-confidence and personal growth.

Meeting Activities:

During each meeting, participants engage in a variety of activities that are aimed at developing their communication skills. These include:

Table Topics: A spontaneous speaking exercise where participants are given a topic and must present a 1-2 minute impromptu speech.

Speeches: Members deliver prepared speeches on topics of their choice, focusing on honing their presentation and delivery skills.

Speech Evaluations: Each speaker receives constructive feedback from an evaluator, which helps them improve their future speeches.

Meeting Evaluations: Evaluators, including the timer, ah-counter, grammarian, and general evaluator, provide feedback on the overall structure and flow of the meeting.
